Our Ideals

What Necropanda is about

Whilst the name might seem odd, Necropanda actually came from one of our programmers and his 'Chronicles of the Necro Panda' comics he would write during bug fix sessions. The spirit of the Panda fighting back against its poacher. The idea of fighting back gave us not only the name of our team, but the idea of our first game as well.

Since our first year as a team we took on new members and are working on our main IPs second chapter. Whilst also trying to give back to they people who love playing our games. Everytime to start development we think of how we can make a game people can enjoy in many different ways. After a successful first year together as a team we couldnt wait to get started on continuing our work, especially after the Falmouth University EXPO, where our studio and game recieved an hnourable mention from Fall guys creators, MediaTonic

Our Games

Our Development History

Corruption of Arcana

Corruption of Arcana is a 2.5D tarot themed rogue lite card combat game where you use arcana cards to fight off the corruption that has returned to plague the realm once again.

Bladder Buster

On your biggest pub crawl ever, you must drink as many beers as you can without your bladder exploding. Eat food to sober up but pay attention because the controls change with each drink you have.

Corruption of Arcana Reshuffled

Gather your cards, weapons and potions because the corruption is growing stronger. Push into the heart of the realm and find the source of this terrible plague. Traverse the world in this 2.5D tarot themed RPG card combat game. Find new items and discover new lands. Anything is possible when fortune favors the bold.
