About the game

What is Corruption of Arcana?

                    Corruption of Arcana is an adventure RPG with a unique twist, Tarot cards are the main source of combat.
                    
                    Tarot cards and use them to fight your way across the realm to find the source of the corruption that has grown stronger in its attempt to claim the world. 
                    
                    Meet and fight with the Arcana Knights and earn your place in their ranks, explore the world; Hear, find and read people's stories of the corruptions first attempt on claiming the world. 
                    
                    Find the source of rumors and end this plague on your home, one way or another.
